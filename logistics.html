Logistics

FedEx Logistics & Shipping Solutions

FedEx is a globally trusted logistics provider offering fast, secure, and scalable shipping solutions for eCommerce sellers, manufacturers, wholesalers, and enterprises. We help businesses utilize FedEx Express shipping with overnight, 2-day, and time-definite delivery options for both domestic and international shipments. Our services include worldwide shipping to 220+ countries with complete customs documentation, regulatory compliance, and clearance assistance. We manage freight transportation including LTL and FTL shipping, palletized cargo, oversized goods, and heavy commercial shipments.

For online sellers, we integrate FedEx shipping with platforms like Amazon, Shopify, and eBay, enabling automated order processing, tracking, and returns handling. We also support Amazon FBA shipments with labeling, repackaging, and warehouse compliance. Same-day delivery solutions, emergency courier services, and temperature-controlled cold chain logistics for pharmaceuticals and perishables are fully managed. Reverse logistics, pickup scheduling, and smooth import-export coordination ensure reliable global supply chain operations with real-time shipment visibility.

DHL Logistics & Shipping Solutions

DHL is a world leader in international logistics, express delivery, and supply chain management, providing reliable shipping solutions across global markets. We assist businesses with DHL Express shipping services including same-day, next-day, and time-definite deliveries for urgent shipments. Our solutions cover international shipping to over 220 countries with expert customs clearance and trade documentation support. DHL freight services include air, ocean, and road transportation for bulk cargo, oversized shipments, and enterprise logistics needs.

ECommerce businesses benefit from seamless marketplace integrations, automated shipment tracking, delivery notifications, and simplified return management. We also handle FBA shipments to Amazon warehouses worldwide with proper packaging and compliance setup. Cold chain logistics for temperature-sensitive goods such as pharmaceuticals and food items are supported with advanced monitoring systems. Warehousing, inventory handling, and distribution solutions ensure efficient end-to-end supply chain management for businesses scaling globally.

UPS Logistics & Shipping Solutions

UPS provides reliable logistics, parcel delivery, and supply chain solutions designed for businesses requiring secure and efficient shipping worldwide. We enable express and expedited shipping services including overnight and scheduled time-definite deliveries for critical shipments. Our international shipping solutions cover 220+ countries with customs clearance assistance and duty estimation support. UPS freight services include air, ocean, and ground transportation for bulk shipments, industrial cargo, and oversized goods.

We integrate UPS with major eCommerce platforms to automate order fulfillment, shipment tracking, and return processing. Amazon FBA shipping support ensures proper labeling, packaging compliance, and bulk shipping optimization. UPS cold chain solutions provide specialized handling for temperature-sensitive products. Reverse logistics and returns automation help businesses manage customer returns efficiently. Warehousing, distribution management, and supply chain optimization services ensure smooth logistics operations from storage to final delivery.

USPS Shipping & Logistics Services

USPS offers cost-effective and dependable shipping solutions ideal for small businesses, eCommerce sellers, and enterprises operating within the U.S. and internationally. We assist with Priority Mail, Priority Mail Express, First-Class Mail, Retail Ground, and Media Mail services depending on delivery speed and budget requirements. International shipping to 180+ countries includes customs handling and documentation support for seamless delivery. Our eCommerce shipping solutions integrate USPS with platforms such as Amazon, Etsy, Shopify, and eBay, enabling automated tracking, insurance, and return label generation.

Businesses benefit from discounted shipping rates, prepaid return options, and efficient parcel handling. USPS services also include PO Box setup, mail forwarding, certified mail, and proof-of-delivery tracking for important business documents. Lightweight parcel shipping, subscription deliveries, and nationwide logistics support make USPS a reliable and economical solution for domestic and cross-border commerce.

Shiprocket Shipping & Logistics Solutions

Shiprocket is an AI-powered logistics platform designed to simplify shipping operations for Indian eCommerce and D2C brands. We provide access to 25+ courier partners with automated courier selection based on cost, delivery speed, and serviceability. Our services include nationwide coverage across 29,000+ pin codes and international shipping to 220+ countries with customs support. Businesses can offer COD and prepaid shipping with automated reconciliation and fraud protection features.

Shiprocket integrates seamlessly with Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Shopify, WooCommerce, and other platforms for centralized order management. Fulfillment services include warehousing, inventory storage, pick-pack-ship automation, and same-day or next-day delivery options. Reverse logistics and smart return management minimize RTO losses. Branded tracking pages, SMS and WhatsApp notifications, shipping analytics, and cross-border shipping via Shiprocket X help businesses optimize delivery performance and customer experience.

DTDC Courier & Logistics Services

DTDC is one of India’s most established courier and logistics networks, offering reliable domestic and international shipping solutions. We provide express, standard, and premium courier services with same-day and next-day delivery options across India. International shipping to 220+ countries includes customs clearance assistance and door-to-door delivery support. DTDC offers specialized eCommerce logistics including COD shipping, reverse logistics, warehousing, fulfillment, and last-mile delivery integration.

Businesses benefit from bulk shipping solutions, dedicated account management, and competitive enterprise pricing. Document courier services ensure secure delivery with tracking and proof-of-delivery confirmation. Air and surface transport options allow flexible logistics planning based on urgency and budget. With widespread coverage and real-time tracking systems, DTDC enables secure and cost-effective shipping operations for growing businesses.

Gxpress Ocean Freight & Shipping Solutions

Gxpress provides specialized ocean freight forwarding services designed for cost-efficient global trade and bulk transportation. We manage Full Container Load (FCL) shipments for large-volume cargo and Less-than-Container Load (LCL) solutions for smaller consignments requiring shared container space. Door-to-door, port-to-port, and port-to-door shipping services ensure seamless coordination between sea freight and inland transportation. Our team handles customs clearance, import-export documentation, and international trade compliance to avoid delays.

We support eCommerce, wholesale, and manufacturing businesses with bulk shipment planning and global distribution logistics. Specialized cargo handling includes heavy machinery, industrial goods, hazardous materials, and temperature-sensitive shipments. Secure packaging, container loading optimization, and real-time tracking provide visibility throughout transit. With competitive freight pricing and strong global carrier partnerships, Gxpress ensures reliable and economical ocean shipping worldwide.
