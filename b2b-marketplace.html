B2B Marketplace

India Mart Seller Services

IndiaMART is India’s largest B2B marketplace, connecting manufacturers, wholesalers, exporters, and service providers with verified buyers across industries. Selling on IndiaMART requires more than basic registration — it demands optimized company profiles, product catalog structuring, keyword-rich listings, professional RFQ handling, and fast response management to generate consistent inquiries. We assist with seller onboarding, documentation, trust seal optimization, and catalog creation to improve search visibility within IndiaMART’s internal ranking system. Our strategy focuses on keyword positioning, competitor benchmarking, lead filtering, and response optimization to improve inquiry-to-order conversion rates.

From managing buyer leads and quotations to improving supplier ratings, subscription upgrades, and premium listing placements, we ensure your business stands out among competitors. We also streamline communication workflows, automate follow-ups, monitor account health metrics, and refine pricing strategies to attract high-intent buyers. Whether you aim to dominate domestic B2B demand or strengthen your export pipeline, our IndiaMART management solutions are designed for scalable lead generation and long-term supplier credibility.

Amazon Business Growth Solutions

Amazon Business provides access to enterprise buyers, bulk purchasers, institutions, and corporate clients across domestic and global markets. Success on Amazon Business requires strategic account setup, GST and tax compliance, optimized B2B listings, bulk pricing configuration, quantity discounts, and enhanced brand positioning. We help sellers create professional storefronts, implement backend keyword strategies, manage Sponsored Ads for B2B visibility, and optimize catalog structures for bulk buyers. Our approach includes inventory planning, FBA configuration for business buyers, automated pricing strategies, and Buy Box optimization to maximize order volume.

We also manage performance metrics, compliance requirements, account health monitoring, and suspension risk prevention. By leveraging analytics, A/B testing, and conversion tracking, we improve lead quality and repeat business transactions. Whether targeting SMEs, procurement departments, or institutional buyers, our Amazon Business solutions are built to increase order size, improve profitability, and establish long-term B2B partnerships.

Alibaba Global Seller Management

Alibaba is the world’s largest B2B wholesale marketplace, connecting suppliers with international buyers across industries. To succeed on Alibaba, sellers must optimize their storefront, complete Gold Supplier verification, structure product listings with high-converting B2B keywords, and actively manage RFQs to secure qualified leads. We assist with business verification, Trade Assurance setup, SEO-optimized product content, professional media integration, and ranking improvements within Alibaba’s search ecosystem. Our services include Keyword Advertising (PPC), bid optimization, buyer targeting, and lead management strategies to increase inquiry quality and response rates.

We streamline quotation processes, negotiation workflows, logistics coordination, export compliance, and inventory planning to support cross-border trade. Additionally, we monitor IP protection, counterfeit risks, store quality scores, and account health metrics to prevent policy violations. With a structured lead generation and conversion framework, we help businesses expand internationally and build sustainable B2B export revenue on Alibaba.

Through data analysis, pricing optimization, and competitive positioning, we enhance lead quality and conversion performance. From initial onboarding to global buyer negotiations and account performance tracking, our solutions help businesses expand exports and build trusted international trade relationships.
